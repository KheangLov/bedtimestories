Thomas and the New World

That summer, Thomas’s family move from a big, busy city to a quiet, tiny village. He soon discovers things which make him feel scared, uneasy, uncomfortable, and even confused. He forges a friendship with his neighbor, Theo, and learns to adapt to his new world.

Stories in this world

Thomas and the Mystery Sounds

Thomas hears mystery sounds while playing outside of his new house. With his mom, they try to find out where the sounds are coming from.

The Best Ice Cream in the World

It’s hot and Thomas wants to have some ice cream. There’s none at home so he goes to Theo’s house. There, he learns how to make “the best ice cream in the world.

The Fishing Challenge

Thomas thinks that he is the best fisher ever, but Theo doesn’t believe him. They agree to do a fishing challenge to prove who’s the best fisher.

The Carrot Teeth

Theo teaches Thomas how to harvest carrots. He eats many of them. The next day, he has gum ache. He blames Theo, until he finds out the truth.

It All Gets Too Much

While shopping, Thomas is surprised that people know about his tooth problem. At the party, more people talk about him. It’s all too much for him so he runs away.
